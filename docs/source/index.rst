Metrima documentation
=====================

Metrima is a no-dependency math library for Python, designed to provide
a wide range of mathematical functions and utilities with ease of use and
efficiency in mind.

Complete with fixed-point arithmetic, time and weight units, and other mathematical utilities, Metrima aims to be a comprehensive solution for developers and mathematicians alike.

Coming soon, detailed documentation and examples will be provided to help you get started with Metrima and make the most out of its features.
